NutriSnap Automatic Renewal Terms

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Scope

These terms apply only to the NutriSnap weekly auto-renewable subscription.

2. Renewal Authorization

  1. By confirming the weekly subscription, you authorize Apple to charge your Apple ID for the next renewal period.
  2. Apple may charge the renewal fee within 24 hours before the current subscription period ends.
  3. If you do not cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, the subscription will automatically renew.

3. How to Cancel

On your iPhone, open Settings → tap Apple ID → select Subscriptions → find NutriSnap → select Cancel Subscription.

After cancellation, your benefits remain active until the end of the current paid period, and the subscription will not renew after that period ends.

4. Billing and Refunds

  1. Apple handles billing, renewal charges, and refunds.
  2. RevenueCat is used to identify subscription status, but it does not replace Apple's billing system.
  3. If you need a refund, please use Apple's official refund process.

5. Price Changes

Any future price change will take effect in the next subscription period after Apple applies the new price.
