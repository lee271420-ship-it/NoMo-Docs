Welcome to NoMo. This agreement is entered into between you and Guo Jueliang regarding the NoMo membership service.

1. Product Description

NoMo is an anti-motion-sickness assistant app that runs locally on iOS devices. The app does not require account registration, does not store data on a server, and all functions run locally on the user's device.

This app does not collect, store, or upload any personal user data.

2. Membership Types and Benefits

NoMo offers the following payment plans:

  • Weekly Membership: CNY 15.00 per week (auto-renewable subscription)
  • Lifetime Membership: CNY 148.00 (one-time purchase)

Membership unlocks the full anti-motion-sickness assistant features and advanced display functions. The specific features are subject to what is shown inside the app.

3. Payment Methods

  1. All payments are processed through the Apple App Store.
  2. The app uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status.
  3. Lifetime Membership is a one-time purchase and does not involve auto-renewal.

4. Refund Policy

All payments and refunds are handled by Apple. If you need a refund, please submit it through Apple's official channels.

5. Disclaimer

NoMo is an auxiliary utility app and is not a medical device or medical product. It is intended only to help relieve discomfort that may occur during travel and does not constitute medical advice or a promise of treatment.

If you have severe motion sickness or other health issues, please consult a qualified physician.
