Auto-Renewal Service Agreement

By enabling the auto-renewal service, you agree to the following terms:

1. Subscription Services

NoMo offers a "Weekly Pro" subscription. After confirmation of purchase, the payment will be charged to your iTunes Account.

2. Renewal Rules

  • Automatic Renewal: To ensure uninterrupted service, your subscription will automatically renew 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Cancellation: You can cancel the auto-renewal at any time. Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Select NoMo → Cancel Subscription.
  • If you do not cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, the subscription will renew automatically.

3. Billing & Refunds

  • Payments and billing are handled by Apple. NoMo uses RevenueCat to verify subscription status but does not store your payment information.
  • For refund requests, please contact Apple Support directly.
