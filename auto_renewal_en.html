This agreement applies to users who purchase the NoMo Weekly Membership (CNY 15.00 per week).

This agreement is entered into between you and Guo Jueliang.

1. Automatic Renewal Description

  1. The Weekly Membership is an auto-renewable subscription product.
  2. By confirming your subscription, you authorize Apple to automatically charge the fee for the next week (CNY 15.00) from your Apple ID account within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.
  3. If you do not cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, the subscription will automatically renew.

2. How to Cancel on iOS

To cancel, follow these steps on your iPhone:

Open Settings → tap Apple ID → select Subscriptions → find NoMo → select Cancel Subscription.

After cancellation, your benefits remain valid through the current paid period, and the subscription will not renew after it expires.

3. Billing and Refunds

  1. Charges and billing are handled by Apple.
  2. The app uses RevenueCat to identify subscription status, but it does not store your payment information.
  3. If you need a refund for any charged amount, please apply through Apple's official refund channels.

4. Price Changes

Any future price changes will take effect in the next subscription period.
