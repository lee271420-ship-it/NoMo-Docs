Last Updated: February 17, 2026

This agreement applies to users who purchase the NoMo Weekly Membership (15.00 CNY/week).

This agreement is entered into between the user (hereinafter "You") and Guo Jueliang (hereinafter "We" or "the Developer").

I. Auto-Renewal Description

  1. The Weekly Membership is an auto-renewable subscription product.
  2. By confirming your subscription, you authorize Apple to automatically deduct the fee for the next week (15.00 CNY) from your Apple ID account within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period.
  3. If you do not cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, the system will automatically renew the subscription.

II. Cancellation Method (iOS)

To cancel, please follow these steps on your iPhone:

Open Settings → Tap Apple ID → Select Subscriptions → Find NoMo → Select Cancel Subscription.

Upon cancellation, your benefits within the currently paid period remain valid, and the subscription will not be renewed after expiration.

III. Billing & Refunds

  1. Payments and billing are handled by Apple.
  2. The application uses RevenueCat for subscription status identification but does not store your payment information.
  3. If a refund is required for deducted fees, please apply through official Apple refund channels.

IV. Price Changes

Any future price adjustments will take effect in the new subscription cycle.
