NoMo User Agreement

1. Purpose of Service

NoMo is a utility tool designed to help alleviate motion sickness (carsickness, seasickness) during travel by providing visual synchronization. It is NOT a medical device and does not provide medical treatment.

2. Subscription & Membership

  • Weekly Subscription: Auto-renews unless canceled.
  • Lifetime Membership: A one-time purchase for permanent access to Pro features.
  • RevenueCat is used for subscription management.

3. Refunds

All payments are processed by Apple. Refund requests must be made through official Apple channels.
