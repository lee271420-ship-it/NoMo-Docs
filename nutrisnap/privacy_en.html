NutriSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

NutriSnap is designed to work with as little account data as possible. Depending on how you use the app, we may process:

  • Profile and plan information you enter, such as age, sex, height, weight, goals, and preferences.
  • Meal photos, scan results, edits, notes, and meal history stored locally on your iPhone by default.
  • An anonymous installation identifier used to enforce the free-scan allowance and protect the service.
  • Meal analysis requests you choose to send, which may include the photo, locale, timestamps, goal, and related metadata needed to generate nutrition estimates.
  • Apple Health data you authorize us to read, such as steps, active energy, workouts, and sleep, if you connect HealthKit.
  • Subscription and entitlement status from Apple and RevenueCat so we can unlock paid features and restore purchases.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate meal estimates, save your journal, and show your nutrition trends.
  • Provide personalized guidance based on your goals and optional Apple Health data.
  • Manage paid features, free-scan limits, entitlement checks, and restore purchases.
  • Respond to support requests, maintain security, and prevent abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Sharing and Storage

Most of your app data stays on your device. When you choose to scan a meal, your photo and related metadata may be sent to our backend and AI service providers to produce a nutrition estimate. Our backend may store anonymous scan records and analysis results, but not the full image file. Apple processes App Store payments, and RevenueCat helps us identify your subscription status. We do not sell personal or health data.

4. Your Controls

  • Delete local data from Settings inside the app.
  • Revoke camera, photo, or Health permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Use Restore Purchases to recover an active subscription.
  • Contact support to request deletion of server-side anonymous records where applicable.

5. Retention and Security

Local meal data remains on your device until you delete it. Anonymous server-side records may be retained as needed to operate the service, prevent abuse, and support your account state. We use reasonable safeguards, but no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Children

NutriSnap is intended for general wellness use. If you are under the age of majority in your location, please use the app with a parent or guardian and do not provide personal data without proper consent.
